San Antonio Pets Alive!

San Antonio Pets Alive's mission is to focus on saving the lives of the dogs and cats already on the euthanasia list at the city shelter.

Wings of Rescue

Wings of Rescue flies endangered pets from high intake and/or high-kill shelters to no-kill shelters from where they have all been adopted into loving homes.

San Antonio Humane Society

San Antonio Humane Society's mission is to protect and improve the lives of dogs and cats by providing shelter, care, adoption, rescue, spay and neuter programs, and community education.

Greater Good

GreaterGood.org works to protect people, pets, and the planet.

San Antonio Animal Care Services

Animal Care Services’ mission is to encourage responsible pet ownership by promoting and protecting the health, safety, and welfare of the residents and pets of San Antonio through education, enforcement, and community partnership.

Animal Defense League

The Animal Defense League's mission is to enhance the quality of life for animals by providing needed medical care, including spaying and neutering. Food, shelter, and safety are also provided – along with compassion and attention.
