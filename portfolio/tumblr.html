problem

Introduction

Tumblr is a popular social media platform that allows users to find and engage with a wide variety of content. Over the course of a semester, my team and I worked with the client to understand how users engage the Tumblr platform and to identify areas for improvement.

process

Research Methods

To understand how users find and engage content they enjoy, our team designed and conducted several studies to uncover potential pain points and any other useful insights, which we could use to form findings and recommendations.

Interaction Map

We mapped out all possible interactions a user would do on the platform.

Interviews

We conducted four one-hour interviews with members of Tumblr's target audience. We developed two overarching research questions that focused on discovery and engagement, as well as an interview protocol and a series of questions that address our research objectives. Each member of the team conducted one interview, which were recorded and later transcribed and coded.

Comparative Analysis

We identified seven to eight different key features, which we used to identify some of Tumblr's key direct, indirect, parallel and analogous competitors. We compared how different competing social networks implemented certain features and evaluated how effective they performed on its respective platforms.

Surveys

We designed, piloted and deployed a survey assess how members of Tumblr's target audience engaged with social media. We had 98 respondents who successfully completed the survey.

Heuristic Evaluation

Our team conducted an individual expert heuristic evaluation based on Jakob Nielsen's 10 heuristic criteria.

Usability Tests

We designed and currently undergoing usability testing with five different participants. Still in progress.
