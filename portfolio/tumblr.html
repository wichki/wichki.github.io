Overview

Research Methods

To understand how users find and engage content they enjoy, our team designed and conducted the following studies to uncoverr potential pain points and any other useful insights, which we could use to form findings and recommendations.

Interaction Map

We mapped out all possible interactions a user would do on the platform. This method gave our team a better understanding how Tumblr works.

Interviews

We conducted four one-hour interviews with Tumblr's target audience. We developed two research questions that focused on discovery and engagement, as well as an interview protocol and a series of questions that address our research objectives. Each member of the team conducted an individual interview, which were recorded and fully transcribed.

Comparative Analysis

Based on previous conversations with the client, we identified seven to eight different key features, which we used to identify some of Tumblr's key direct, indirect, parallel and analogous competitors. We compared how different competing social networks implemented certain features and evaluated how effective they were within its context. Based on the data, we generated a series of findings and recommendations.
