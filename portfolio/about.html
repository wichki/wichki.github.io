about me

I am an empathetic individual who is not only interested in crafting compelling and accessible stories, but also in designing intuitive, equitable and user-centered products, systems and services.

I have experience with user experience research and design methods. I have taken coursework in interaction, graphic and web design; programming; and business-related courses including economics, statistics and marketing. I am currently a part of a graduate student team designing and conducting UX research and usability tests for Tumblr.

This past summer, I volunteered as a UX researcher and designer for a nonprofit in Cape Town, South Africa, where we consulted on communication strategy and internal processes.
